# Sakshi and the Festival of Lamps - Chunk 04

Prince Aarav noticed Sakshi not because of her clothes, but because she bowed respectfully to the palace cooks and thanked the guards. They spoke about books, rivers, and the duty of serving people before power. For the first time in years, Sakshi felt seen for who she truly was. As drums thundered and dancers spun, the first midnight bell rang. Sakshi remembered the warning and ran down the marble steps. In her hurry, one embroidered jutti slipped off and remained on the palace staircase.
