# Sakshi and the Festival of Lamps - Chunk 05

By the final bell, the carriage was gone, the lehenga had changed back, and Sakshi stood again in her old cotton dress. The next morning, Prince Aarav announced he would search for the person whose kindness had moved him. He carried the single jutti through every neighborhood, asking each household to let someone try it. At Sakshi's home, her stepsisters pushed forward first, but the jutti would not fit either of them.
