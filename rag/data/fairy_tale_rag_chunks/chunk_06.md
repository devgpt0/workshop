# Sakshi and the Festival of Lamps - Chunk 06

Sakshi's stepmother tried to hide her in the kitchen, yet Aarav heard a familiar voice humming a prayer song. When Sakshi stepped out, the jutti fit perfectly. Aarav asked not for proof of wealth, but one question: would she help him build a fair kingdom where no child slept hungry. Sakshi said yes with steady confidence. Soon they were married in a ceremony lit by thousands of diyas, with music from shehnai and laughter from the whole city. Sakshi invited her stepfamily too, choosing compassion over revenge.
