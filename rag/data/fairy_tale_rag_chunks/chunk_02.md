# Sakshi and the Festival of Lamps - Chunk 02

As the great Diwali festival approached, the royal palace announced a grand celebration for all families in the kingdom. The prince, Aarav, wished to meet someone who understood both compassion and courage. Sakshi longed to see the palace lights just once, but her stepmother laughed and piled extra work before her. When the household left for the celebration, Sakshi sat alone in the courtyard beside an unlit diya. An elderly woman appeared near the tulsi plant, dressed like a wandering storyteller.
